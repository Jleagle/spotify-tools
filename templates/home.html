Spotify Helper

Spotify Helper is designed to offer features that Spotify its self does not offer, or try to improve on features Spotify does offer.

Why do we need access to your spotify account?

To read or write data to your account we need to be given permission.
We currently do not keep any data about you or your account.

Other Services

A collection of other Spotify related websites we like.

  • JQBX lets you play & listen to music in sync with friends or public groups - https://app.jqbx.fm/
  • Every Noise is an ongoing attempt at an algorithmically-generated, readability-adjusted scatter-plot of the musical genre-space - http://everynoise.com/

Open Source

Spotify Helper is an open source project written by Jim Eagle in Go.
If you wish to run the website locally or contribute, see the GitHub page.
